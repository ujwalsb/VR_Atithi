Tamil Nadu A city of Culture

Tamil Nadu, located in southern India, is renowned for its ancient temples and rich cultural heritage. The state is home to some of the most iconic Dravidian-style temples, known for their towering gopurams (gateway towers) and intricate stone carvings. Notable temple sites include:
1. **Brihadeeswarar Temple (Thanjavur)**: A UNESCO World Heritage site, this 11th-century temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is a masterpiece of Chola architecture.
2. **Ramanathaswamy Temple (Rameswaram)**: Known for its longest temple corridor, this temple is one of the holiest shrines in Hinduism.
3. **Mahabalipuram (Mamallapuram)**: A UNESCO World Heritage site, Mahabalipuram is known for its rock-cut temples, cave sanctuaries, and the famous Shore Temple built by the Pallavas.
