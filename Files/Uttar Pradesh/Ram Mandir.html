Ram Mandir

The Ram Mandir, located in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India, is a significant Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ram. It is built on the site believed to be the birthplace of Lord Ram, an important figure in Hinduism. The temple's construction began after a landmark Supreme Court ruling in 2019 that resolved a long-standing dispute over the land. The temple is being constructed in a grand architectural style, symbolizing peace, unity, and devotion. The foundation stone was laid by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in August 2020, and it is expected to be a major religious and cultural center upon completion.
