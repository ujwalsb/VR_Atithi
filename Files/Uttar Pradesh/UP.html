Uttar Pradesh (UP) is a state in northern India, known for its rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and large population. It is India's most populous state, with cities like Lucknow (the capital), Varanasi, and Agra. UP is home to the iconic Taj Mahal, sacred rivers like the Ganges, and important religious sites for Hindus, Buddhists, and Jains. The state's economy is driven by agriculture, manufacturing, and services. It plays a key role in India's politics due to its size and number of parliamentary seats.
